The world of Generative AI is exploding, and staying ahead means knowing your tools. For every Generative AI engineer, mastering the right frameworks is crucial. Here's a quick rundown of the powerhouses shaping the future:

*   **PyTorch**: The go-to for flexibility and research, offering a rich ecosystem for image and text generation.
*   **TensorFlow**: An end-to-end platform, robust for deploying deep learning models in various generative tasks.

*   **LlamaIndex**: Essential for connecting custom data with LLMs, empowering knowledge assistants and RAG applications.
